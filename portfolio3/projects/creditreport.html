    Full Credit Report


    Background

    Full credit was a major feature roll-out for SavvyMoney's 2017 product roadmap. It gave SavvyMoney competitive edge with most competitors in the space, who already had the credit report offering.


    My Role

    I led the UI vertical of the feature rollout, from defining requirements, designing the web and mobile interface to collaborating with partnership team to run email and marketing campaigns for new feature release.


    Process

    The process started with educating myself more about credit reports by looking into sample reports by TransUnion, parsing through the xml's to understand its structure and learning more about attributes that make up the credit report. I also established competitive benchmark by looking at various competitor products. Next up was creating various concepts and designing the UI.


    Impact

    The feature was well received by the partners who were very excited to roll the feature out their members. We saw a hike in engagement and soon, the credit report pages became the second-most visited pages across the product, only after credit score. 


    Follow up

    Later, we launched a printable version of the report in Q1 2018 which the user's could download and use it in branches to discuss their financial prospects.


    Learnings

    Before this project, I barely knew anything about credit report and the depth of information contained in it. The project also emphasized on the importance of hierarchy - of information shown to the users, of different visual treatments that can be used to establish it; and importance of consistency of navigation across the product.


    Overall, it was a very humbling experience to realize that over 300,000 users use the pages designed by me as a reference to their financial decisions.
