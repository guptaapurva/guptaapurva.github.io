About





HELLO WORLD! I am a UX designer, future enthusiast and maker!

My design philosophy is that I believe that design doesn’t exist in a bubble but in an ecosystem. It should not only consider users but also underlying technology and business needs. Every design results in a consequence - positive or negative; and a good design takes care of them.

What interests me most is designing for emerging technologies. I have worked on exploring interactions and use-cases of digital note taking for Microsoft [Link] and envisioning future of self driving police car for Motorola Solutions [Link]

These and many other of my projects have inspired me to explore and rethink the interactions and use-cases for connected devices (IoT) and voice user interfaces in the context of home, as my thesis project. [Link]

I can talk at length about design processes or the next big thing. Sounds interesting? Let’s talk!
