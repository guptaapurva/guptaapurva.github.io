Supply Chain Dashboard - JDA

Supply chain management dashboard for Master Planners

The project was out in collaboration with JDA Software, a leader in global supply chain management. The aim of the project was to create a collaborative dashboard for master planners in the supply chain industry to aid them in efficient decision making. A user-centered design approach was followed. Based on the user interviews, personas and use-cases sent by the client, insights were drawn with respect to the roles, responsibilities and tasks of master planners. These insights were used to showcase role specific data i.e performance based indexes (KPI's), trends, alerts/warnings, deadlines, etc. on a platform that is seamlessly integrated for both desktops and other portable devices. Wireframes and mockups were created and a funcional prototype was test built for evaluation.

Understanding the requirements

Planners need to constantly take care of facility profiles on map, fiscal calendar, scorecard.They need to see a lot of information at the same time and need to collaborate a lot with others for inquiries and feedback.

Final Design

The final iteration was the improvement over the previous ones. This iteration improves upon information access and interactions with the system. This design for converted into a limited functionality prototype for user evaluation. The following features were introduced and implemented in the prototype.

Map Visualisation

Similar interactiona as google maps. Exceptions are flagged over the map which can also be toggled to a list view

Collaborator

A chat client was introduced to the system with the addon to accept/reject proposals and send requests.

My Sapce

Added a personal space for the planners to place their “favorites”, “Watch-list”, and “Notes”with the option of adding additional widgets.

Plan Calender

Introduced plan calendar to mark tasks, meetings, etc. A floating screen on the right shows important dates.

Ticker

To make the dashboard dynamic, we have introduced a ticker at the bottom which runs the latest news of the company.

Reports

Planners can review, mark, download or share financial reports or customer profiles using reports tab.
