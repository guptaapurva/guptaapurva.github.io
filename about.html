about me

Ollo! I am Apurva. A product designer by day, tinkerer by night and unapologetic foodie in between ;)

I currently work as a UX designer at the fintech startup SavvyMoney Inc. Being new to the domain, its fun and interesting to learn that the things that affect us the most financially, are so convolated and rarely explained. I have been striving to make such concepts easy to understand and use, for all, with accessible design.

I am a true believer in the power of consistency and it has become my design mantra. By being consistent, you get enough of an open field to explore, while being grounded within boundaries that keeps the entire product together. It is this belief that has helped me successfully set-up the Octane design system.

Outside of work, I learn and teach laser-cutting at Robot Garden, a community maker space. I love food. And thus, try new cuisines and new recipes. You can check some of my food doodles on Instagram.

Want to know more or just grab a cup of coffee? Please reach out. :)
Cheers!!
