About

Namaste _/\_

A User-Experience designer & researcher and amateur media artist, I am a Final year MS-HCI student from Georgia Institute of technology. With over 5 years of experience in design exploration, I specialise in Interaction design with a focus on Emerging Technologies and Motion graphics. I combine this design package with an oversight into Management of Technology.

I perceive Design as a new dimension for improvement and advancement. Design education has not only made me explore more but has increased my empathy towards looking at the "big-picture". A food-lover, sport enthusiast and animated movies buff, I am most-likely to be found at Starbucks sipping my favourite Mocha!.
